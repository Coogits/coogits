    Arithmetic

    Arithmetic is the branch of mathematics that deals with the study of numbers and the basic operations performed on them. These operations include addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Arithmetic forms the foundation of mathematics and is essential for everyday calculations, numerical reasoning, and the development of more advanced mathematical concepts.
